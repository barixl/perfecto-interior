Introduction to Perfecto Interior

We are interior design specialists with deep expertise in residential and commercial spaces, bringing your dream home to life with a perfect touch.

We care about the communities we serve because it is designers, together with our craftsmen — our architects, designers, site engineers, and project managers — working together at the core of the creative process. We believe that every home deserves a thoughtful, beautiful, and functional design.

At Perfecto Interior, our team of passionate designers and project managers work together to deliver flawless interiors — bringing style, comfort, and elegance into every space we touch.

Our History

Over the years we have received appreciation and recognition across West Bengal — from Kolkata to Medinipur — by recognizing our thoughtful and client-first approach to interior design.
2024

Founded (expanded) Perfecto Interior was established with a single mission — to deliver perfect interior design solutions within every client's budget. Starting from Purba Medinipur, we quickly expanded our reach to Kolkata, Ghatal, and beyond.

2024

First Projects Completed our first residential projects in Kolaghat and Medinipur, earning 100% client satisfaction and strong word-of-mouth referrals.

2025

Expansion Opened our second office at Ripon Street, Kolkata — 700016, bringing our services closer to Kolkata's growing residential and commercial market.

2025

Growing Strong Now serving clients across Purba Medinipur, Paschim Medinipur, Kolkata, and nearby districts with a full team of expert designers and craftsmen.
